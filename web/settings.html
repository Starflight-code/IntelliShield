Check-In
If you're feeling unsafe, schedule an hourly check in. Each hour, you'll be prompted to confirm your safety. If you miss a check in, we'll let your emergency contacts know.

Confirmation Check-In
To prevent false alarms, we can send you a notification before we reach out to your contacts. If you don't respond, we'll still reach out automatically on your behalf.
